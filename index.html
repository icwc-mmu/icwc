About

Our research and clinical consortium is helping to redefine chronic wounds research in deep learning.

  • 2018-2020

    Initial Data Collection

    Our initial partnership with NHS Lancashire Teaching Hospitals begins with the collection of 4000 chronic wound images and expert labels.

  • 2020-2021

    FootSnap

    The development of our first prototype chronic wound detection system - FootSnap. The system is tested in a 6 month multi-center clinical evaluation at 2 UK NHS hospitals, followed by the largest reliability study for chronic wound deep learning research to date.

  • 2021-2022

    Conference Proceedings

    Authors / editors of the first major conference proceedings on chronic wound classification and segmentation, conducted in conjunction with the International Conference on Medical Image Computing and Computer Assisted Intervention (MICCAI).

  • 2023

    Consortium Expansion

    We expand our international partnership with clinical experts in New Zealand, Saudi Arabia, and India, resulting in the gathering of new and highly diverse datasets.
